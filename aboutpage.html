eARC: Transforming Support Coordination at AFB

Our Challenge

The coordination of support amongst organizational stakeholders, especially during high-activity periods such as large force exercises, presents a substantial challenge. The current process is heavily reliant on a 24-page PDF Andersen Reception Checklist (ARC), email communications, and manual tracking methods inefficient and prone to errors.

❮ ❯

Our Vision

eARC aims to modernize and streamline this process by transforming the legacy ARC into a dynamic web application. By facilitating direct, digital interactions between requesting and supplying organizations, eARC simplifies negotiations and contract finalizations, thereby enhancing efficiency and traceability.

Key Features of eARC

  • Intuitive Interface: A responsive web application that tailors the experience to each user, minimizing confusion and jargon.
  • Smart Alerting: Automated notifications ensure that the right points-of-contact receive and respond to requests promptly.
  • Two-Way Communication: Built-in messaging allows for transparent negotiations and amendments to support contracts.
  • Visual Dashboards: Real-time tracking of support requests and progress, reducing the need for frequent meetings.
  • Secure Data Management: Proper classification levels are maintained for data storage, with archival capabilities for historical analysis.
